## TRICKS AND PATTERNS

### 9× Table Trick
- Digit sum always = 9
- 9 × 4 = 36 (3 + 6 = 9)
- 9 × 7 = 63 (6 + 3 = 9)

### 11× Table Pattern
- Up to 11 × 9, digits repeat: 11, 22, 33, 44, 55, 66, 77, 88, 99

### 12× Table = Double 6× Table
- 12 × 3 = 36 (double of 6 × 3 = 18)
- 12 × 5 = 60 (double of 6 × 5 = 30)

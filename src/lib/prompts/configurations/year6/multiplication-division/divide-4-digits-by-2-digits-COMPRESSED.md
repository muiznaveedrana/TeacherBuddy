## LONG DIVISION METHOD

### Example: 432 ÷ 18

```
      2 4
    ------
18 ) 4 3 2
    -3 6    (18 × 20 = 360, but try 18×2=36)
    -----
      7 2
     -7 2   (18 × 4 = 72)
     -----
        0
```

**Answer**: 24

### Chunking Method
432 ÷ 18
- Take 10 × 18 = 180, leaves 252
- Take 10 × 18 = 180, leaves 72
- Take 4 × 18 = 72, leaves 0
- Total: 10 + 10 + 4 = 24

## INTERPRETING REMAINDERS

**Need**: 12 minibuses (must round UP!)

## LONG MULTIPLICATION METHOD

### Example: 234 × 56

```
      ²¹²
    2 3 4
  ×   5 6
  -------
  1 4 0 4  (234 × 6)
1 1 7 0 0  (234 × 50)
---------
1 3 1 0 4
```

**Step 1**: 234 × 6 = 1,404
**Step 2**: 234 × 50 = 11,700
**Step 3**: Add: 1,404 + 11,700 = 13,104

## GRID METHOD (Alternative)

|×|200|30|4|
|-|-|-|-|
|50|10,000|1,500|200|
|6|1,200|180|24|

Total: 10,000+1,500+200+1,200+180+24 = 13,104

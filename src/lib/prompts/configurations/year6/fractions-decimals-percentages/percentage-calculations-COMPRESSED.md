## QUESTION TYPES
**Q1**: Find 50% of 240
**Q2**: Find 25% of 180
**Q3**: Find 10% then use to find other %
**Q4**: Percentage increase (£200 + 15%)
**Q5**: SATs reverse percentage problem

## KEY METHODS

### Finding 50%
**50% of 240**
- 50% = half
- 240 ÷ 2 = 120

### Finding 25%
**25% of 180**
- 25% = quarter
- 180 ÷ 4 = 45

### Finding 10%
**10% of 350**
- 10% = divide by 10
- 350 ÷ 10 = 35

**Then 30%** = 35 × 3 = 105

### Percentage Increase
**£200 + 15%**
1. Find 15%: 200 ÷ 10 = 20, then 20 × 1.5 = 30
2. Add: 200 + 30 = £230

### Percentage Decrease
**£80 - 20%**
1. Find 20%: 80 ÷ 5 = 16
2. Subtract: 80 - 16 = £64

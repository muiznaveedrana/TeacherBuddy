## KEY OPERATIONS

### Adding Decimals
**3.456 + 2.789**

- Add as normal
- = 6.245

### Subtracting Decimals
**8.54 - 3.267**
- Line up: 8.540 - 3.267
- Subtract: = 5.273

### Multiplying by Powers of 10
**4.56 × 100**
- Shift decimal point 2 places RIGHT
- = 456

### Dividing by Powers of 10
**456 ÷ 100**
- Shift decimal point 2 places LEFT
- = 4.56

## KEY METHODS

### Adding/Subtracting (Different Denominators)
**2/3 + 3/4**
1. Find common denominator: 12
2. Convert: 8/12 + 9/12
3. Add: 17/12 = 1⁵/₁₂

### Multiplying Fractions
**3/4 × 2/5**
- Multiply tops: 3 × 2 = 6
- Multiply bottoms: 4 × 5 = 20
- Simplify: 6/20 = 3/10

### Dividing by Whole Number
**3/4 ÷ 2**
- Same as: 3/4 × 1/2
- = 3/8

### Mixed Numbers
**1²/₃ + 2¹/₄**
1. Convert: 5/3 + 9/4
2. Common denominator: 12
3. 20/12 + 27/12 = 47/12 = 3¹¹/₁₂

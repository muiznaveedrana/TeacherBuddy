## CURRICULUM (Year 3)
- **Range:** Numbers 0-1000 (3-digit mental addition)
- **Key strategies:**
  - Bridging through 10/100 (e.g., 47 + 6 = 47 + 3 + 3 = 53)
  - Partitioning (e.g., 234 + 45 = 234 + 40 + 5 = 279)
  - Near doubles (e.g., 35 + 36 = 35 + 35 + 1 = 71)
  - Adding 9/99 (add 10/100 then subtract 1)
  - Compensation (e.g., 56 + 29 = 56 + 30 - 1 = 85)
- **Key misconceptions:**
  - Not bridging correctly through 10/100
  - Forgetting to compensate when using near numbers
  - Mixing up strategies mid-calculation

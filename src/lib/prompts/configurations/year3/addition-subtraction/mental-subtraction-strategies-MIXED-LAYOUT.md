## CURRICULUM (Year 3)
- **Range:** Numbers 0-1000 (3-digit mental subtraction)
- **Key strategies:**
  - Counting back (e.g., 53 - 8: count back 8 from 53)
  - Subtracting through 10/100 (bridging)
  - Partitioning (e.g., 234 - 45 = 234 - 40 - 5 = 189)
  - Subtracting 9/99 (subtract 10/100 then add 1)
  - Compensation (e.g., 56 - 29 = 56 - 30 + 1 = 27)
  - Find the difference (counting up)
- **Key misconceptions:**
  - Not bridging correctly through 10/100
  - Forgetting to compensate when using near numbers
  - Subtracting smaller from larger incorrectly

## CURRICULUM (Year 3)
- **Range:** Numbers 100-999 (three-digit numbers)
- **Key skills:** Read and write numbers in numerals AND words
- **Key vocabulary:** hundred, hundreds, and, one, two, three, four, five, six, seven, eight, nine
- **UK English spellings:** "four hundred and fifty-six" (NOT American style "four hundred fifty-six")
- **Key concepts:**
  - Numbers can be written as digits (456) or words (four hundred and fifty-six)
  - "and" is used between hundreds and tens (e.g., "three hundred and twenty-five")
  - Place value chunks: hundreds, tens, ones
- **Key misconceptions:**
  - Confusing -teen and -ty (e.g., "thirteen" vs "thirty")
  - Reversing digits when hearing number words (hearing "sixteen" and writing "61")
  - Missing "and" in British English number words
  - Zero placeholders: 306 = "three hundred and six" (NOT "three hundred and zero six")

## QUESTION TYPE OPTIONS

### Q1 OPTIONS (Fluency - Pick ONE):
- **Words to digits**: Given number words → write as digits
- **Digits to words**: Given digits → write in words
- **Match pairs**: Match digits to words (3 pairs)

### Q2 OPTIONS (Fluency - Pick ONE):
- **Fill missing word**: "five hundred and _____ -two" (fifty)
- **Complete number words**: Write numbers in full word form
- **Read and write**: Read number word, write digit (or vice versa)

### Q3 OPTIONS (Application - Pick ONE):
- **Price tags**: Shop items with prices in words/digits
- **Score boards**: Sports scores shown in words
- **Population signs**: Town population in words/digits

### Q4 OPTIONS (Application - Pick ONE):
- **Story context**: Character reads/writes numbers in context
- **Number labels**: Label items with number words
- **Ticket numbers**: Event tickets with numbers in words

### Q5 OPTIONS (Reasoning - Pick ONE):
- **Spot the mistake**: Incorrect word-number conversion
- **True or False**: Statements about reading/writing numbers
- **Odd one out**: Find the number that doesn't match the pattern
